💰 BudgetPro — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

BudgetPro collects only the financial data you manually enter (transactions, budgets, categories). Free users store data locally on their device only. Premium users may optionally sync data to Firebase.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the budgeting features of the app. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Advertising

Free users see ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can opt out in your device settings under "Ads".

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — cloud storage for Premium users
  • Google AdMob — advertising for free users
  • Google Play Billing — subscription management

5. Data Security

All data transmitted to Firebase is encrypted in transit and at rest. Local data is stored securely on your device.

6. CCPA (California Users)

California residents may request deletion of their personal data by contacting us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

BudgetPro is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
